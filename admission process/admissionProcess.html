Entrance Exam:

  • JELET (Admission in 1st year)
  • VOCLET(Lateral Entry)

How to apply?:

visit west bengal state council of technical education official website and apply for jelet or voclet exam as per your eligibility and preference.

For more information visit wbscte.in

Eligibility criteria:

  • All the candidates looking for admission should have passed the 10th exam with a minimum of 35% aggregate marks that too without adding the marks secured in the additional subject.
  • Aspirants must have studied Mathematics and Physical Science (Science) as mandatory subjects at class 10th level.
